Hello!

I'm a senior at Cal Poly San Luis Obispo studying Computer Engineering. After graduating, I'll be joining Broadcom as an R&D IC Design Engineer in their ASIC Product Division.

Previously, I interned at Marvell Technology in Santa Clara, where I developed FPGA models to validate evaluation boards for their high-speed ComPHY chips and built an automation framework for power module characterization.
