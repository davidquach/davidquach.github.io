Hello!

I’m a senior Computer Engineering student at Cal Poly San Luis Obispo. In the near future, I’ll be joining Broadcom as an R&D IC Design Engineer within their ASIC Product Division.

I’m particularly interested in all things chip design, having developed a deep-rooted passion for RTL design in my undergraduate courses and furthering my experience at Marvell Technology, where I worked on FPGA development. As I enter my career, I’m excited to explore backend ASIC development and work with industry-level tools.

While I work primarily on digital ICs, I’m also interested in embedded development for audio applications, real-time operating systems (RTOS), and computer graphics.
