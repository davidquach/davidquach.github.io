Hello!

I’m a senior Computer Engineering student at Cal Poly San Luis Obispo.

I’m particularly interested in both chip design and embedded programming. I developed a deep-rooted passion for RTL design in my undergraduate courses and furthered that experience at Marvell Technology, where I designed FPGA models to validate evaluation boards for Marvell's ComPHY chips. As I enter my career, I’m excited to explore backend ASIC development and work with industry-level tools.

At the same time, I really enjoy embedded development, especially in areas like audio applications, real-time operating systems (RTOS), and computer graphics.
